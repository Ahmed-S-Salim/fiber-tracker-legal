Privacy Policy

ASAblocker · Last updated July 8, 2026

ASAblocker does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The short version

ASAblocker is a privacy-focused web browser that blocks ads and trackers. It runs entirely on your device. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no data is ever sent to us — because there is no “us” server to send it to.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect names, emails, locations, contacts, identifiers, or usage data. We have no servers that receive your information.

Your browsing data

  • Your history, cookies, and site data stay on your device, managed by the system web engine (WebKit) — the same technology Safari uses.
  • You can erase all of it any time from Settings → Clear browsing data.
  • When you visit a website, your connection goes directly to that website over its own HTTPS encryption. ASAblocker sits between you and ad/tracker servers only to block them — it does not log or transmit what you browse.

Ad & tracker blocking

Blocking happens locally: a content-blocking rule list stops requests to known ad/tracker hosts, and an on-device script hides first-party ads on pages you open. No browsing information leaves your device to make this work.

Third-party websites

ASAblocker lets you open third-party sites (such as YouTube, Facebook, and streaming services). Those sites have their own privacy policies and data practices, which we do not control. This policy covers only the ASAblocker app itself.

Children’s privacy

ASAblocker does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will appear here inside the app and at its public web address.
