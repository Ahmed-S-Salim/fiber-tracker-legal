Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Fiber Tracker Pro (“the app”) is an offline field‑technician tool. It is built so that your data stays on your device.

Data we collect: none.

The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no user accounts.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — devices, work orders, customers, earnings, and expenses — is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. It is never uploaded to us or any third party. Deleting the app removes this data from your device.

Device permissions

  • Camera — only to scan barcodes and capture work‑order/receipt photos on your device.
  • Photos — only to import images you choose for scanning.
  • Location — only, with your permission, to tag where a device was installed; coordinates are stored locally with that record.

All of the above are processed on‑device and are never sent off the device.

Third parties

The app makes no network calls to our servers. If you optionally sign in to your employer’s work‑order portal from within the app, that connection uses your own credentials and is governed by that provider’s policy; we never receive or store those credentials.

Children

The app is intended for working technicians and is not directed to children. It collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change. Continued use of the app means you accept the current policy.
