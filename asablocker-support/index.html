FAQ

Some ads still appear. Ad blocking is best-effort — YouTube and Facebook actively fight blockers, so a few ads may slip through. Updates improve this over time.

How do I clear my data? Settings → Clear browsing data. Everything stays on your device.

Background & Picture-in-Picture. Enable them in Settings → Media to keep audio/video playing when you leave the app.
